# 2 Wheels Ahead

Illustration project for Ottawa’s 2 Wheels Ahead, a courier-owned bicycle courier company. The character illustration appears on promotional materials and the bicycle chain logo is the primary signage on their fleet of cargo bikes that deliver documents, office supplies, food and flowers around Ottawa’s downtown core.
