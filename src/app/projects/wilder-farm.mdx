# Wilder Farms

Located on the Quebec/Ontario border is Wilder Farms. Some big tomato hoop houses, fields of peppers, lettuce and flowers this small farm delivers CSA baskets from Montreal to Ottawa. Working with my mentor and lettering expert Luke Norrad, I did the illustrations and we created these handbills for Wilder Farms.
