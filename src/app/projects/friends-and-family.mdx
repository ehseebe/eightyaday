> # Friends and Family
>
> ## work, illustration, tactile
>
> Friends & Family is a cozy spot for the best pizza and wine in Portland, Maine. I’ve had a lot of fun working with Friends & Family for a few projects over the last couple of years. I’ve designed these little foodie characters that appear on hoodies, flags, stickers, and the menu at the restaurant. From pizza and wine to charcuterie objects like sausage and olives, these little happy-dancing characters are a blast to imagine and create.
