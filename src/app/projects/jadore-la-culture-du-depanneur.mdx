> # J'adore la culture du dépanneur
>
> ## featured, illustration, tactile
>
> Another ode to the dépanneur. Imagined, designed, and printed in Montreal. I decided to print these totes on a little thinner of a bag than my other totes so that it can be folded up and put in your back pocket when you go buy a pack of darts, two 710s, 4 muffins and a pepperoni stick. I gifted one to the dep guy and he loves it.
