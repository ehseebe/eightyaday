> # Wildfire Undies
>
> ## tactile, illustration, work
>
> Based out of Asheville, North Carolina I did a bunch of collaborations with AE to make some pretty sweet lingerie and beachwear. Along with doing the wildfire logo, I did a “what’s in your pockets” type illustration, a 420 weed illustration and a beach goth print for some swimwear. These turned out super cool and till this day remains one of the funnest projects I ever worked on.
